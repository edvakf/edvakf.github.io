This interactive presentation shows how each country in the world came to win the Olympic medals (all medals combined).

Tap the circular button at the bottom right of the page, or you can select the specific year from the selection button on the top right.

You can also hover a mouse over a country/region to see the histogram of its medals over time.

(Medals of Soviet Union are merged to Russian, and West German to German)
